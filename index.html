About the seminar

Lost in the Myths of Time is a Stanford University summer seminar sponsored by the Bing Overseas Programs. The seminar is comprised of fourteen undergraduate students from varying academic backgrounds brought together under the tutelage of Professor Elaine Treharne and accompanied by graduate assistant Matthew Aiello. Having met up in the U.K. at the end of June, the group spent three weeks studying Anglo-Saxon language, landscape, and identity in its many iterations.
